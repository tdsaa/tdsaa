    The CTARA family is like no other !!


    We are a group of technical professionals from multi-disciplinary backgrounds sensitized towards the problem of rural India.

    We are involved in various sectors such as Agriculture, Water (Water resource management, Water quality), Nutrition, Energy, Policy, etc., working for the bottom 80% population of the pyramid.

    Our role as the Technology & Development Students-Alumni Association (TDSAA) is to strengthen the bonds with CTARA and each other. We aim to help our CTARA family members to excel in knowledge, learning, association, and work, and we inspire them to act as forces for good in communities and the world.

    We feel it as a duty to give back to our beloved institution by taking help of the learning that we acquired from it; and to enrich it further by our renewed and fruitful experiences..

    It's time we act together, now and in synchrony, to make our society and environment a better place to live.

    ____________ Diversity & Belonging _____________


    While we are connected in our shared mission to better the world, we also recognize that we are not all the same.

    We are a fabric woven together of strands of Red and blue and yellow - different life experiences, different cultures, and diverse backgrounds. All five fingers are different in shape and size, but they make up the complete hand together.

    In the Alumni Association, we are committed to fostering welcoming communities that give voice to differing perspectives, opinions, and viewpoints, as long as they are productive and respectful of all people's dignity.

    Our commitments

    We engage with you in ways that you always remember CTARA. We keep CTARAs mission alive in you and through you. We tell your stories, great and small because everyone has one to share. We maintain an Alumni database so you can build relationships with fellow members of the CTARA family. We remind you that you belong to something bigger. We commit to valuing your perspective and diverse opinions.

    Objectives

    01

    Communication

    To communicate regularly with the Alumni and keep them mutually informed about Alumni's achievements and CTARA.

    02

    Knowledge sharing

    To provide a continuous platform for knowledge and experience sharing for alumni and present students of CTARA.

    03

    Developing bonds

    To foster strong bonds between alumni, students, and the Institute, and create a network enabling them to remain engaged with their alma mater and help shape its future through the association's programs and services.

    04

    Strong Networking

    To enhance Networking platforms for alumni events that provide an opportunity to meet with employers, industry professionals, etc.
